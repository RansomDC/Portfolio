About

A great deal of my working experience has come from working with marine mammals, birds, fish and invertebrates. That is how I met my wife, and we still bond over our love of wildlife to this day.
But working in aquariums takes a heavy physical toll, and after almost 8 years I knew that if I continued, I wouldn't be able to pick up my future children. So I decided that I needed a change.

I am a recent graduate of The Tech Academy’s Software Developer Boot Camp. I have finished my courses in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, SQL, Django, C#, .NET and several other frameworks. I am excited to move forward with my career, and to use the skills that I've gained in this process!
